Hi, I'm Pedro!
Also known as Pelucio, a freelancer and illustrator from Brazil. My art is heavily influenced by the vibrant cartoon styles of the 80s and 90s. Nostalgic and contemporary elements are part of my work.
Ever since I was little, art has been my jam. The magic of bringing life to inanimate stuff making everything look way cooler has always fascinated me.
So, in some point, making art started to be something I've working on. Digital art, creating brand logos for clothes and stuff like that along my journey till now, but always craving for seeking out new opportunities to share my art to the world.
